The Russian Cultural Club aims to share knowledge and news about Russia, Russian culture, the Russian language, and the culture of Russophone countries.
The club began as a study group for STEM+ Russian students, and developed into Gatton's only club led entirely by juniors. We now meet weekly and are
proud of our strong weekly attendance and wonderful club members. Our meetings consist of well-researched presentations, snacks, and dances.
